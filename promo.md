## Full Description

**ASMRify** is a standalone Windows desktop tool that batch-processes voice recordings and TTS clips into polished ASMR or hypnotic audio — no DAW, no plugins, no audio knowledge needed.

Pick one of four built-in sound presets:
- **Soft-Spoken ASMR** — warm, intimate, with gentle reverb and subtle stereo movement
- **Whispered ASMR** — breathy, close-mic feel with heightened high-frequency detail
- **Relaxing Hypnosis** — smooth, slightly slowed, with theta binaural beats and slow panning
- **Deep Mesmerization** — deep, rich, with gamma pulse binaural beats and immersive spatial movement

Add optional layers: binaural beats (Alpha / Theta / Gamma), background noise (brown, pink, white), spatial panning, pitch wobble, echo and chorus, warmth and saturation.

Drop a folder of files in, hit Start, get processed audio in seconds per file. Supports WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, M4A, AAC, WMA, OPUS as input.

**Requires FFmpeg on PATH.** Easy one-click installer included: https://reactorcore.itch.io/ffmpeg-to-path-installer

**Binaural safety:** headphones required. Not for use by people with epilepsy or pacemakers.
