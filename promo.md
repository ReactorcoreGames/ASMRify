## Full Description

**ASMRify** is a standalone Windows desktop tool that batch-processes voice recordings and TTS clips into polished ASMR or hypnotic audio as a layperson friendly program.

The biggest cool thing is the ability to add automatic natural sounding spacial ear-to-ear effects - like the famous 3Dio Binaural Microphone where the ASMR artist can do that tingly trick where they get really close to one ear and whisper into it, then move to the other ear, making you feel like you're surrounded and very intimate.

This program can do that thing without you needing an expensive 3Dio. You can feed it any voice recording audio file and it will create that smart effect using app magic with a single click.

### Main Modes

When you use the program, you get to choose one of main modes:

- **Soft-Spoken ASMR** — warm, intimate, with gentle reverb and subtle stereo movement
- **Whispered ASMR** — breathy, close-mic feel with heightened high-frequency detail
- **Relaxing Hypnosis** — smooth, slightly slowed, with theta binaural beats and light echo
- **Deep Mesmerization** — deep, rich, with gamma pulse binaural beats and immersive deep echo

### Addons

Then on top of that, you get to add optional extras that further alter the audio in awesome ways:

- Tempo/Speed, Voice Pitch, Intensity
- Room Ambience Style
- Spatial panning (Ear-to-Ear movement)
- Noise (brown, pink, white, grey, green, etc.)
- Binaural beats (Alpha / Theta / Gamma)
- Pitch wobble, warmth and saturation

All controls offer super-easy radio buttons and simple sliders to tweak them, making it easy even for a total beginner to make their audios sound awesomely enhanced.

### Usage

Drop your audio files into any folder, run the program, choose input/output folders, choose effects and hit Start.

Depending on how big your audio files it can take a few seconds or a few minutes to process.

Supports WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, M4A, AAC, WMA, OPUS as input.

Creates WAV and MP3 as output.

**Requires FFmpeg on PATH.** Easy one-click installer included: https://reactorcore.itch.io/ffmpeg-to-path-installer

Headphones required. Otherwise you won't properly hear some effects, like the ear-to-ear stuff or binaurals.
